Return & Refund Policy

At GEOID STUDY CENTRE , we strive to provide the best possible educational resources and digital learning experiences. However, as most of our content and courses are digital, we have a clear return and refund policy outlined below.

1. Digital Courses: Once you have purchased and accessed a course, refunds will not be issued as digital materials cannot be returned. Please read the course details carefully before purchasing.

2. Duplicate Payment: In case of any accidental duplicate transaction, please contact us within 7 days. After verification, we will initiate a full refund to your original payment method.

3. Technical Issues: If you experience problems accessing a course due to a technical error on our side, we will ensure the issue is fixed promptly or provide an alternative solution.

4. Subscription Cancellation: You can cancel your subscription anytime. However, refunds are not applicable for the current billing period once the content has been accessed.

5. Physical Materials: If applicable, damaged or incorrect physical study materials can be replaced upon valid proof within 7 working days of delivery.

If you have any refund-related queries, please reach us at support@eduvision.in with your payment receipt and details.
